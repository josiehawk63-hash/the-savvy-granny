# Ready-to-Use Pinterest Pins 📌

How to pin each one manually (takes ~2 min each):
1. On Pinterest, click **Create** → **Create Pin**.
2. **Upload the image** — the vertical photos are on your Mac at
   `~/the-savvy-granny/public/images/recipes/<name>-pin.jpg`
   (or live online at `https://thesavvygranny.com/images/recipes/<name>-pin.jpg`).
3. Paste the **Title** and **Description** below.
4. Paste the **Destination link** (this is what sends people to your site!).
5. Pick a **board**, then **Publish**.

Suggested boards to create first: **Cheap Dinners**, **Budget Breakfasts**, **Frugal Soups & Sides**, **Budget Family Meals**, **Money-Saving Kitchen Tips**.

### Pinterest tips for traffic
- **Pin consistently** — a few pins a day beats 50 pins once. Spread them out.
- **Make multiple pins per recipe** over time (different images/titles) — more shots at going viral.
- **Join group boards** in the budget/recipe niche for extra reach.
- **Use keywords** people actually search: "cheap dinners," "budget meals," "meals under $5."
