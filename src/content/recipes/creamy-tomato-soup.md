ingredients:
  - "2 tbsp butter or olive oil"
  - "1 onion, diced"
  - "3 cloves garlic, minced"
  - "2 cans (14 oz each) crushed or whole tomatoes"
  - "2 cups vegetable or chicken broth"
  - "1 tsp sugar"
  - "1/2 cup milk, cream, or a splash of evaporated milk"
  - "Salt and pepper to taste"
  - "Optional: fresh basil or a pinch of dried basil"
instructions:
  - "Melt the butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes."
  - "Stir in the garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant."
  - "Add the tomatoes (with their juice), broth, and sugar. Bring to a simmer and cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally."
  - "Blend the soup until smooth — use an immersion blender right in the pot, or carefully transfer to a countertop blender in batches."
  - "Return to low heat and stir in the milk or cream. Season with salt, pepper, and basil. Warm through and serve."
notes: "Grilled cheese on the side turns this into the ultimate cheap comfort meal. A splash of evaporated milk (cheaper than cream) makes it luxuriously creamy."
