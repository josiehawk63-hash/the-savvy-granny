ingredients:
  - "8 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs (the cheapest cut!)"
  - "1 tbsp oil"
  - "1 tsp salt"
  - "1 tsp paprika"
  - "1 tsp garlic powder"
  - "1/2 tsp black pepper"
  - "1/2 tsp dried thyme or Italian seasoning (optional)"
instructions:
  - "Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Pat the chicken thighs very dry with paper towels — this is the key to crispy skin."
  - "Rub the thighs all over with the oil, then sprinkle on the salt, paprika, garlic powder, pepper, and herbs."
  - "Arrange skin-side up on a baking sheet, leaving space between them."
  - "Bake for 35-40 minutes, until the skin is golden and crisp and the juices run clear (internal temp 175°F/80°C)."
  - "Let rest 5 minutes before serving."
notes: "Bone-in, skin-on thighs are far cheaper than breasts and almost impossible to dry out. Roast some potatoes or veggies on the same pan to make it a full meal."
