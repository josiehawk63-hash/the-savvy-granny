ingredients:
  - "1 can (14 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed"
  - "1/2 tsp cumin"
  - "1/2 tsp garlic powder"
  - "8 flour tortillas"
  - "2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar or a Mexican blend)"
  - "1 tbsp oil or butter for the pan"
  - "Optional: salsa, sour cream, or hot sauce to serve"
instructions:
  - "In a bowl, lightly mash the black beans with the cumin and garlic powder — leave them a little chunky."
  - "Spread the bean mixture over half of each tortilla, top with a generous sprinkle of cheese, and fold over."
  - "Heat a little oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook each quesadilla 2-3 minutes per side until golden and crisp and the cheese has melted."
  - "Cut into wedges and serve with salsa, sour cream, or hot sauce."
notes: "Add leftover rice, corn, or chopped peppers to stretch them further. These reheat well in a dry pan to stay crispy."
