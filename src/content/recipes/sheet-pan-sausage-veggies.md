ingredients:
  - "1 lb smoked sausage, sliced into coins"
  - "1.5 lbs potatoes, cut into bite-size chunks"
  - "1 onion, cut into wedges"
  - "3 cups any vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots — whatever's cheap)"
  - "3 tbsp olive oil"
  - "1 tsp garlic powder"
  - "1 tsp paprika"
  - "Salt and pepper to taste"
instructions:
  - "Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C)."
  - "On a large sheet pan, toss the potatoes and onion with 2 tbsp oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Spread in a single layer and roast for 15 minutes."
  - "Add the sausage and vegetables to the pan, drizzle with the remaining oil, and toss to combine."
  - "Roast for another 18-20 minutes, until the potatoes are tender and everything is golden at the edges."
  - "Serve hot, straight from the pan."
notes: "Use whatever vegetables are on sale or wilting in your fridge — this recipe is endlessly flexible. Line the pan with foil for zero cleanup."
