ingredients:
  - "1 lb (450g) spaghetti or any long pasta"
  - "1/4 cup olive oil"
  - "6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced"
  - "1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)"
  - "1/2 cup grated parmesan, plus more to serve"
  - "Salt and black pepper, to taste"
instructions:
  - "Bring a large pot of well-salted water to a boil and cook the pasta until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining."
  - "While the pasta cooks, warm the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-low heat. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes and cook gently until the garlic is golden and fragrant — about 2–3 minutes. Don't let it burn."
  - "Add the drained pasta to the skillet along with a splash of the reserved pasta water. Toss well."
  - "Remove from heat, add the parmesan, and toss again, adding more pasta water a little at a time until you have a silky sauce."
  - "Season with salt and plenty of black pepper. Serve hot with extra parmesan."
