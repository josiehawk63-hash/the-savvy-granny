ingredients:
  - "2 cups elbow macaroni"
  - "2 cups water"
  - "1.5 cups milk"
  - "2 cups shredded cheddar cheese"
  - "2 tbsp butter"
  - "1/2 tsp salt"
  - "Pinch of pepper and (optional) mustard powder or paprika"
instructions:
  - "Add the macaroni, water, milk, and salt to a pot. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, stirring often so the pasta doesn't stick."
  - "Reduce to a simmer and cook, stirring frequently, for 10-12 minutes until the pasta is tender and most of the liquid is absorbed into a creamy sauce."
  - "Turn off the heat. Stir in the butter, then the shredded cheese a handful at a time, until melted and silky."
  - "Season with pepper and a pinch of mustard powder or paprika if you like. Serve right away — it's creamiest fresh."
notes: "Stir often while it simmers — that's the secret to creamy, not sticky. Shred your own cheese from a block; it melts smoother and costs less than pre-shredded."
