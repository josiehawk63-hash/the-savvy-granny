ingredients:
  - "3 very ripe bananas, mashed"
  - "1/3 cup melted butter or oil"
  - "1/2 cup sugar (or brown sugar)"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 tsp vanilla (optional)"
  - "1 tsp baking soda"
  - "Pinch of salt"
  - "1 cup all-purpose flour"
  - "1 cup rolled oats"
instructions:
  - "Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line or grease a 12-cup muffin tin."
  - "In a large bowl, mix the mashed bananas with the melted butter, sugar, egg, and vanilla."
  - "Stir in the baking soda and salt, then gently fold in the flour and oats until just combined."
  - "Divide the batter among the muffin cups, filling each about three-quarters full."
  - "Bake for 18-22 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool a few minutes before removing."
notes: "The riper the bananas, the sweeter the muffins — those spotty bananas no one wants are perfect here. Add a handful of chocolate chips or chopped nuts if you have them."
