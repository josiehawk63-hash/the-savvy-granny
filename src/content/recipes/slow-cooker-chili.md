ingredients:
  - "1 lb ground beef (or turkey)"
  - "1 onion, diced"
  - "3 cloves garlic, minced"
  - "2 cans (14 oz each) kidney or pinto beans, drained"
  - "1 can (14 oz) black beans, drained"
  - "1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes"
  - "2 tbsp chili powder"
  - "1 tsp cumin"
  - "1 tsp salt"
  - "1 cup water or broth"
instructions:
  - "Brown the ground beef with the onion in a skillet over medium heat, breaking it up, until cooked through. Drain excess fat. (Short on time? You can skip browning and add it raw — just stir well.)"
  - "Add the garlic and cook 1 minute, then transfer everything to the slow cooker."
  - "Add the beans, crushed tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, salt, and water. Stir well."
  - "Cover and cook on LOW for 6-8 hours (or HIGH for 3-4 hours)."
  - "Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve with cheese, sour cream, or crackers."
notes: "This freezes beautifully and tastes even better the next day. Stretch it further with an extra can of beans — they're cheaper than meat and just as filling."
