ingredients:
  - "1 cup peanut butter"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "1 egg"
instructions:
  - "Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment."
  - "In a bowl, mix the peanut butter, sugar, and egg until it forms a smooth dough."
  - "Roll into 12 balls and place on the baking sheet. Press a criss-cross pattern into each with a fork."
  - "Bake for 9-11 minutes, until set at the edges but still soft in the middle. They firm up as they cool."
  - "Cool on the pan for 5 minutes before moving — they're delicate when warm."
notes: "That's it — no flour needed! Add a splash of vanilla or a pinch of salt if you have it. Use crunchy peanut butter for texture."
