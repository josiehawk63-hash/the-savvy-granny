ingredients:
  - "2 tbsp olive oil"
  - "1 onion, diced"
  - "2 carrots, diced"
  - "2 celery stalks, diced"
  - "3 cloves garlic, minced"
  - "1 tsp cumin"
  - "1 cup dried brown or green lentils, rinsed"
  - "1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes"
  - "6 cups vegetable or chicken broth"
  - "Salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lemon to finish"
instructions:
  - "Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion, carrots, and celery and cook until softened, about 6–8 minutes."
  - "Stir in the garlic and cumin and cook for 1 minute until fragrant."
  - "Add the lentils, diced tomatoes, and broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer."
  - "Simmer uncovered for 30–35 minutes, until the lentils are tender."
  - "Season with salt and pepper, finish with a squeeze of lemon, and serve. It's even better the next day."
notes: "Freezes beautifully — portion into containers for easy lunches all week."
