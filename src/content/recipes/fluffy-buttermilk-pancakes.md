ingredients:
  - "1.5 cups all-purpose flour"
  - "2 tbsp sugar"
  - "1 tbsp baking powder"
  - "1/2 tsp salt"
  - "1.25 cups milk (plus 1 tbsp lemon juice or vinegar to make 'buttermilk')"
  - "1 egg"
  - "3 tbsp melted butter or oil"
instructions:
  - "Stir the lemon juice or vinegar into the milk and let it sit for 5 minutes to make a quick buttermilk."
  - "In a bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt."
  - "In another bowl, whisk the milk mixture, egg, and melted butter."
  - "Pour the wet ingredients into the dry and stir just until combined — lumps are fine. Don't overmix, or the pancakes will be tough."
  - "Heat a lightly greased skillet over medium heat. Pour about 1/4 cup batter per pancake. Cook until bubbles form on top, then flip and cook 1-2 minutes more."
  - "Serve warm with syrup, fruit, or a pat of butter."
notes: "No buttermilk needed — the milk-and-lemon trick works perfectly and costs nothing extra. Make a double batch and freeze extras for fast weekday breakfasts."
